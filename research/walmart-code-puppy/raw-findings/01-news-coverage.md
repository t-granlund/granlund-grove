## Bloomberg Article
- **URL:** https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-01/walmart-caps-usage-of-an-ai-tool-for-employees-after-high-demand
- **Title:** Walmart Caps Usage of an AI Tool for Employees After High Demand
- **Date:** June 1, 2026
- **Author:** Jaewon Kang
- **Key findings:**
  - Walmart is limiting staff usage of Code Puppy due to high demand
  - Code Puppy was "developed in-house at Walmart"
  - Rolled out to employees as part of retailer's push to incorporate AI
  - Staff also have access to Claude and ChatGPT
  - Walmart offering set amount of "tokens" per employee (previously unlimited)
