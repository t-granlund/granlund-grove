## 3. Outdoor Cap Factory Locations

### Manufacturing Locations
Outdoor Cap manufactures through suppliers in facilities located throughout:
- **Bangladesh**
- **China**
- **Vietnam**
- **Sri Lanka**

**Sourcing Staff Locations:**
- U.S.
- **Hong Kong**
- **China**

**In-house designers collaborate with designers in Asia.** Some supplier partnerships have continued for 40 years.

**Certifications:**
- C-TPAT (Customs Trade Partnership Against Terrorism)

### U.S. Distribution Centers (3 total, 284,000+ sq ft)
1. **Bentonville, Arkansas** — Corporate headquarters, operations center, domestic embroidery, largest DC. Serves Heartland, Upper Midwest, Upper East.
2. **Rancho Cucamonga, California** — Opened 1998, newest DC. Serves Western region, Alaska, Hawaii. Near port for faster transit.
3. **Dallas, Texas** — Serves Southwest and Southeast. 1-2 day shipping during peak seasons. Services Promotional Products, Team Dealer, and Retail customers.

**Note:** The user mentioned Ohio. The official Outdoor Cap website only lists 3 DCs (AR, CA, TX). However, the Compass wholesale site (Legendary Headwear) lists: Bentonville AR, Compton CA, and Zanesville OH. This may be a separate distribution network for the wholesale/team division.

### Technology Stack
- **ERP/Magento:** Both outdoorcap.com and wholesale.legendaryheadwear.com run on **Magento** (evident from page source: Magento_Checkout, Magento_PageBuilder, Magento_Catalog, Magento_Theme)
- **Compass:** Online ordering tool for wholesale customers at https://wholesale.legendaryheadwear.com/compass
  - Features: Create custom orders, manage & track orders, online approvals, view order messages
  - Services brands: Legendary Headwear, Outdoor Cap, Team MLB
