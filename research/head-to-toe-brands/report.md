## Owned Brands

Head to Toe Brands operates a multi-brand beauty franchise platform with four portfolio concepts:

### 1. The Lash Lounge
- **Category:** Eyelash extensions & brow services
- **Founded:** 2004 by Anna Phillips
- **Website:** [thelashlounge.com](https://www.thelashlounge.com)
- **Booking Site:** [booking.thelashlounge.com](https://booking.thelashlounge.com)
- **Locations:** ~155 (per poidata.io, Nov 2025); 128 shown on live booking locator; corporate site claims "140+ open salons"
- **States:** ~32 states + DC, including AL, AR, CA, CO, FL, GA, IL, IN, KS, KY, LA, MD, MA, MI, MS, MO, NV, NJ, NM, NY, NC, OH, OK, OR, PA, RI, TN, TX, UT, VA, WA, WI
- **Model:** Membership-based recurring revenue

### 2. Bishops Cuts/Color
- **Category:** Hair salon / barbershop (unisex, inclusive)
- **Founded:** 2001 in Portland, OR
- **Website:** [bishops.co](https://bishops.co)
- **Locations:** 41 shops (per live location finder on bishops.co/locations/)
- **States:** 13 states + DC — WA, OR, CA, NV, AZ, CO, TX, MN, IN, OH, GA, NC, VA, DC
- **Model:** Walk-in friendly, à la carte services

### 3. Frenchies Modern Nail Care
- **Category:** Clean, non-toxic nail salon
- **Founded:** 2014 by Guy & Stephanie Coffey in Littleton, CO
- **Website:** [frenchiesnails.com](https://frenchiesnails.com)
- **Locations:** 25+ (per franchise portal data); Riverside press release states "more than 20 locations across 13 states"
- **States:** 13 states (exact list not fully enumerated from public sources)
- **Model:** Membership model, spa-like atmosphere

### 4. Delta Crown Hair Extensions
- **Category:** Luxury hair extensions (membership-driven)
- **Founded:** 2017 by Jenna Bowden
- **Franchise Site:** [franchise.deltacrown.com](https://franchise.deltacrown.com)
- **Acquired by HTT:** November 2025
- **Locations:** Early-stage franchising; first franchise agreements announced May 2026
- **States:** National franchise opportunity launched March–May 2026; current footprint is small (corporate + first franchisees)
- **Model:** Boutique footprint (800–1,000 sq ft), no-tipping team model, recurring revenue

- **Marketing:** HubSpot forms (portalId: 21313054)
- **Analytics:** Jetpack Stats
- **Accessibility:** accessiBe widget
