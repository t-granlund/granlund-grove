## Five Core Fundamentals of Efficient E-Commerce Development
1. Customer-centric
2. Rapid iterations
3. Enhanced usability
4. Alignment with agile SDLC
5. Market adaptability

## Three Anchor Components for Success
1. Communication - facilitate collaboration, knowledge sharing, ensure everyone is on the same page
2. Planning and requirements - efficient resource allocation, risk management, customer needs
3. Testing excellence - identify and fix issues early, improved customer satisfaction

## Practical Example: Eco Home Goods
Tyler used a fictional "Eco Home Goods" company throughout the presentation to demonstrate:
- Creating internal organizational transparency via product development dashboard
- Using Adobe Workfront for task management
- Enhancing product detail pages with eco-certifications and sustainability information
- Testing procedures for new PDP features
- Managing dual-scale project streams (regular sprints + large implementations)
