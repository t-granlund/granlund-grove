### 2. If Researching for Competitive/Industry Analysis

**Key takeaways for outdoor/farm retail sector:**
- Regional players can thrive against national chains through community focus
- Adobe Commerce is the dominant mid-market platform in this sector
- BNPL (Bread/Affirm) is now table stakes for big-ticket outdoor purchases
- In-store services (licenses, archery, pet wash) create stickiness that pure e-commerce can't match
