## Source Gaps and Limitations

1. **Exact Brazil opening year**: No single source states the exact year the first Brazil school opened. Inferred to be ~2015 based on Portela's 2013 career change and 2 years of operating his own school.

2. **Portugal opening date**: Announced for 2020-2021, but no confirmation of first opening date found.

3. **Current Taiwan location count**: Opening confirmed but current count not verified in accessible sources.

4. **Spain current count**: Madrid opened May 2021; no current total found.

5. **Pre-2015 international data**: Limited publicly available data before 2015.
