# School of Rock International Franchise Expansion Research

## Executive Summary

School of Rock, founded in 1998 in Philadelphia, PA, is a performance-based music education franchise. As of March 2025, the brand operates **400+ locations across 17 countries**, with approximately **400 domestic (US) and 91 international locations**. The international expansion accelerated significantly after 2017 under CEO Rob Price, with master franchise agreements driving growth in Latin America, Europe, and Asia.

---

## 1. Brazil Expansion

### Timeline
- **Master Franchisee**: Paulo Portela, former IBM Brazil executive
- **Portela's Career Change**: 2013 — After 26 years at IBM and experiencing burnout, Portela read about School of Rock in a franchise magazine roundup and decided to switch careers
- **First School**: Portela operated his own school for 2 years before signing as master franchisee, suggesting Brazil operations began around **2015**
- **October 2019**: 250th global location opened in Ipanema, Rio de Janeiro — at this time, Brazil had **33 contracted schools** with plans for 117 additional schools (total 150 planned)
- **February 2020**: **21 locations open** in Brazil, with deals signed for 16 more; 3,000+ students enrolled
- **May 2026**: **25 locations** currently operating in Brazil (per POI data)

### Current Brazil Locations (May 2026): 25 total

- Federal District (Brasília): 1

### Key Cities

- Other cities: Ribeirão Preto, São José dos Campos, São Caetano do Sul, Campinas, Jundiaí, Barueri, Indaiatuba, Guarulhos, Niterói, Santana de Parnaíba

## 2. Paraguay, Peru, Chile

### Chile
- **Master Franchisee**: Matias Puga Hamilton
- **First Location Opened**: **2015** in Santiago, Chile (Puga opened his first location 3 years after joining the brand in 2012)
- **Master Franchise Rights**: January 2018 (Chile, Peru, Colombia, Mexico, Paraguay)
- **Current Locations**: **10** (as of Oct 2025)

### Peru
- **First Location Opened**: **2017** — This was Puga's first international launch outside Chile
- **Current Locations**: **2** (as of Oct 2025)

### Paraguay
- **Opening**: **October 2020** in Asunción
- **Franchisees**: Letizia and Julio Troche (guitarist of Paraguayan band Kchiporros)
- **Contract Signed**: December 2019; original plan was early 2020 but delayed by pandemic
- **Enrollment**: 200+ students in first month, peaked at 320 students
- **Current Locations**: **1** (as of Oct 2025)
- **Note**: Paraguay appears to have been developed independently by local franchisees, though Matias Puga also held master franchise rights

## 3. Portugal and Spain (Brazil Team Expansion)

- **Master Franchisee**: Paulo Portela (same as Brazil)
- **Master Franchise Agreements Awarded**: By October 2019
- **First European Location**: Madrid, Spain — opened **May 2021**
  - Located in Pozuelo de Alarcón
  - Owned by Luigi Lubelli, Cristina Fernández, and her brothers
  - First of 28 schools planned across Spain, Portugal, and Brazil markets
  - Planned: 20 schools in Spain, 8 in Portugal (or 28 total across Spain and Portugal)
- **Portugal**: Locations planned to open "later in 2021" per May 2021 article
- **Significance**: This marked School of Rock's first European expansion and 15th country open or in development

## 4. Taiwan Master Franchise

- **Master Franchise Agreement Awarded**: **December 7, 2020**
- **Master Franchisee**: SOR Taiwan Company LTD, led by John Caraccio (20 years retail development experience in Taiwan)
- **First School Opened**: **March 2021** (or October 2021 per some sources) in Taichung City
- **Development Plan**: 20 locations across Taiwan over 10 years
- **Significance**: Taiwan marked the **15th country** open or in development for School of Rock
